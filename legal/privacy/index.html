2. What GymDuels is

GymDuels is a competitive gym app. You sign in with Apple, join a participating gym, log workouts, compete on weekly tower rankings, challenge other members, and (optionally) subscribe to GymDuels Pro. There is no in-app chat.

Gym bots: Participating gyms may include synthetic bot members operated by GymDuels (not real people). Bots appear on towerboards and can be challenged like other members. They are clearly labeled in the app (tower silhouette avatar and “Gym bot” on profiles). Bot workout points do not count toward gym war scores. Bots do not sign in, do not upload photos, and cannot message you.

3. Information we collect

3.1 Account and authentication

  • Sign in: Sign in with Apple.
  • Apple-provided data: When you use Sign in with Apple, we may receive a stable user identifier and, if you choose to share it, your name and email as provided by Apple.
  • Account identifiers: An internal user ID assigned by our authentication provider.

3.2 Gym bot profiles (system-generated)

When bots are enabled for a gym, we create and operate bot profile records that include:

  • Display name and program description (example workout pattern)
  • Tower standings, workout sessions, training levels, and challenge participation (same data categories as human gameplay, generated by our servers)
  • A tower-type avatar (system icon, not a user photo)

Bot profiles are visible to gym members on towerboards and player profiles. They are not linked to Sign in with Apple or any human account. We do not collect personal information from bots; we generate their gameplay data to operate the Service.

3.3 Profile and preferences

  • Display name (visible to other members at your gym(s))
  • Profile photo (optional; visible on towerboards and player profiles)
  • Home gym and optional visiting gym
  • Home city and state (for gym directory browsing)
  • Unit preferences (lbs/kg, miles/km)
  • Appearance preference (light or dark mode — stored on device)
  • Subscription tier and trial end date
  • App Store subscription linkage (when you purchase Pro): Apple transaction identifiers and related subscription status, such as product, environment, expiration, and refund status

3.4 Legal acceptance (age and policies)

When you complete the in-app legal gate, we store:

  • The date and time when you confirmed you are 17 or older (we do not collect your date of birth)
  • The dates and times when you accepted our policies
  • The policy versions you accepted so we can prompt you to re-accept when they change

3.5 Workout and gameplay data

Workout data you log is self-reported (honor system). We do not continuously track your GPS location or verify every exercise in person.

  • Workout sessions: Tower type, exercises, sets, distance, duration, points scored, gym context, timestamps, and optional links to active challenges
  • Routines: Weekly workout templates you save
  • Tower standings: Weekly rank, points, and visibility flags at your gym(s)
  • Challenges: Participants, gym, tower, status, outcomes, and points at resolution
  • Training levels: Per-tower tier, level, and lifetime training XP
  • Achievements: Badges unlocked and unlock timestamps
  • Gym wars participation: Aggregated human member points for paired gym competitions (where enabled)

Much of this information is visible to other members at your gym according to product rules (towerboard, player profiles, challenge history), including bot-generated standings where bots are enabled.

3.6 Reports and moderation

If you report another user or gym bot, we collect:

  • Your user ID (reporter)
  • The reported user’s ID
  • Reason (harassment, cheating, other, or bot issue for gym bots only)
  • Details you provide (required for “other,” minimum 10 characters)
  • Optional context (for example gym ID, challenge ID, source screen)
  • Report status and review timestamps

You can view reports you submitted through our systems; you cannot view reports others filed about you through the app.

3.7 Data stored on your device

  • Offline cache: Profile, gym info, and recent tower snapshots for offline browsing
  • Pending workouts: Solo workouts logged offline and queued until sync
  • Appearance preference: Light or dark mode
  • Local subscription entitlements: StoreKit may keep Apple purchase receipts / entitlements on device

Local data is cleared when you sign out or delete your account, subject to iOS backup behavior.

3.8 Photos

Profile avatars are stored with Supabase Storage. Other members can see your avatar on towerboards and profiles.

3.9 Analytics

We do not currently send gameplay events to a third-party analytics provider.

If we enable an analytics provider, we will update this policy and applicable App Store privacy disclosures before collecting data.

3.10 Advertising (Basic tier)

Basic users (after the 7-day Pro trial, if applicable) may see Google AdMob interstitials in these cases:

  1. Challenge versus — immediately before the challenge versus animation when starting a challenge workout.
  2. Tower publish — when adding a second or later workout session to the weekly towerboard on the same gym-local day (after the first session auto-publishes). Workouts are always saved to your log; only competitive tower points require this extra step for Basic users.

Pro and Pro Trial users do not see these placements in the current configuration.

Google and its partners may collect device and usage data to serve and measure ads under Google’s Privacy Policy.

3.11 Push notifications

If you allow notifications, we store an Apple Push Notification service (APNs) device token on your account and may send transactional alerts such as:

  • You received a challenge
  • A challenge was resolved (won / lost) or expired
  • Training level-ups
  • Weekly towerboard reset summaries
  • A new athlete set your gym as home
  • Gym War matchups and results

You can disable notifications anytime in iOS Settings → Notifications → GymDuels. We do not use push for marketing in the current product.

3.12 Purchases and App Store Server Notifications

When you buy or restore GymDuels Pro:

  • Apple processes payment and holds your payment method; we do not receive your full card number.
  • We may receive purchase and subscription lifecycle information from Apple, including product, transaction identifiers, environment, expiration, and revocation or refund status.
  • We associate purchases with your GymDuels account so Pro access stays consistent across devices.

3.13 Information we do not collect

  • No continuous GPS / location tracking (gyms are selected from a directory)
  • No HealthKit data in the current version
  • No in-app messaging or contact graph beyond gym and challenge context
  • No sale of personal information to data brokers (AdMob data use is governed by Google’s terms)
  • No collection of government ID through the app

4. How we use information

We use information to:

  • Provide the Service — authentication, profiles, workouts, rankings, challenges, training levels, achievements, gym wars
  • Enforce product rules — challenge limits, subscription gates, moderation reports
  • Process legal acceptance — verify age confirmation and policy version compliance
  • Moderate the community — review user reports, remove offensive content, restrict or terminate accounts
  • Operate offline features — cache data and sync pending workouts
  • Improve reliability — debugging, fraud prevention, and (if enabled) privacy-safe analytics
  • Serve ads to Basic users before challenge versus and before optional tower publish (AdMob), where enabled
  • Process and verify subscriptions — StoreKit entitlements and App Store Server Notifications updating Pro eligibility
  • Send transactional push notifications you have allowed
  • Comply with law and respond to valid legal requests
